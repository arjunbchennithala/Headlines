Aim

To Build a community-driven news portal named HeadLines

Introduction
Introduction
  • HeadLines is an online news portal that allows anyone to upload news reports
  • Most of the News reports are uploaded and verified by the users of this system
    • Existing systems
    Existing systems
    • Regular online news portals - Posts news about most of the issues
    • Blog websites - Let anyone to write blog posts about anything
    Disadvantages of existing systems
    Disadvantages of existing systems
  • Regular news portals doesn't allow any outsiders to post any news reports directly
  • It may block some informations from being published
  • Time difference
  • Blog websites can't be used as a news websites because of the posts are not verified
  • Most of the blog sites doesn't have an option to report if there is a problem in a post
    • Proposed system and its advantages
    Proposed system and its advantages
  • It is a hybrid of blog websites and regular news websites
  • It allows anyone to upload news reports
  • The uploaded news will be sent to review by other users before publishing
  • The users who review the news are related to the topic or place that the news is based on
  • Minimum of 5 users must verify it
  • Posts that includes words that are prohibited will hold for approval by admin before peer review
    • Modules
    Modules
  • This system consists of 3 modules for Guest user, Registered user and Admin
  • Guest users are the visitors
  • They can't post news or any comment
  • Registered users are the actual users of the system
  • They are registered using their personal details
  • They can post news and comments
  • They are responsible for verification of other's posts
  • Admin is the one who controls and monitors the system
  • Add or approve any post
  • Take action against users who violate the rules
  • Monitors the website status
